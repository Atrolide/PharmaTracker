About

PharmaTracker is a cloud native web application designed to help pharmacies and individuals keep track of their medicines. It acts as a digital storage space for tracking medications in a pharmacy or a home medicine cabinet. The app is especially useful for elderly users who might find it challenging to manage their medicine supplies. To make it more user-friendly for this audience, the app features a simple and supportive UI.
